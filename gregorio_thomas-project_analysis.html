Spring Lake Elementary Project Analysis

Site Intent

The redesigned Spring Lake Elementary website should provide quick and easy access to the school’s calendar, the school’s policies, easy access to purchasing uniforms and supplies online, and a form for contacting the school with any questions or concerns.

The redesign should also feature the school’s message, accolades, and the technology currently in use. This information will help parents doing research on multiple schools understand what sets Spring Lake Elementary apart from other area schools.

Competitive Analysis

Local area private schools, such as Park Maitland School and Center Academy, have URL’s directly referencing the school in them, which makes the site easier to get back to without needing a bookmark. Spring Lake Elementary is a subdomain of ‘K12.fl.us’. Not only does this add complexity to the URL, but it also makes the URL more difficult to remember without a bookmark.

Some keywords that popped up in research that should be attached to the SEO are “Seminole County schools” and “Compare and Contrast”. This implies users may be looking for the best school in the area.

Targeted Audience

The site’s predominant audience are mothers ages 35-44 (though the audience is as high as 55-64). Based on this information, the site should serve as a companion tool for those with children enrolled. Information such as the school calendar should be displayed prominently on the home page along with contact information (via phone, fax, or online form).

For parents doing research, a dedicated “About Us” section should detail the school’s history, accolades it has received, and technology in use. This section will not only be informational, but also serve as a ‘pitch’ to convince parents Spring Lake Elementary is a great place to have their kids enrolled.
