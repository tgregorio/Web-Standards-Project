Uniforms

Students at Spring Lake Elementary are required to wear school uniforms. Uniforms are a simple, sensible way to increase pride in our school and increase concentration on classroom activities.

School uniforms do not have to be purchased from any particular vendor nor is the school insignia required. Spring Lake Elementary does sell uniforms at all Open Houses and at designated times throughout the year.

Here is a list of suggested vendors:

  • Target
  • K-Mart
  • Wal-Mart
  • Macy's
