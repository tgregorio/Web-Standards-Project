Attendance Policy

In the event your child is absent, you must provide written documentation in order for the absence to be excused.

If a teacher does not receive an excuse not within 5 days of the absence, the absence will remain unexcused.

All work that is missed due to unexcused absences will result in a grade of 0.

Tardies Policy

Students must be in the classroom by 7:50 AM. Arrival after 7:50 will result in the student being assigned a tardy slip.

All work that is missed due to unexcused tardies will result in a grade of 0.

If a student accumulates over 5.5 hours (or 330 minutes) of unexcused time due to tardiness, the student will receive an unexcused absence for one day. All work for the unexcused absence will result in a grade of 0.
